PAYMENT OPTIONS

Preferred Payment Method - ONLINE PAYMENT

  • On our receipt of your completed Site Form, an invoice will be issued via email.
  • Credit Card OR Direct Deposit – Follow the link on the invoice to access our secure credit card OR direct deposit payment option.
  • Invoices need to be paid within seven days.The site will not be allocated to you until payment has been received.
  • On our receipt of your payment, a notification will be sent to you via email.

Secondary Payment Option - CASH OR CHEQUE

  • Drop this form off at Valentine Public School with your cash or cheque payment enclosed.
  • Please make cheques payable to: Valentine Public School P&C.
  • Place the form and payment in an envelope, write ‘Market Day’ and ‘your name’ or ‘business name’ on the front of the envelope.
  • Deposit the envelope into the P&C deposit box, located inside the school office.
  • On our receipt of your payment, a notification will be sent to you via email.
