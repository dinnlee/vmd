LOGISTICS

Access for Setting Up and Packing Up

You will be fully informed of the designated set up/pack up times, access and parking for your site as the date approaches. We kindly request you adhere to the designated times to ensure a smooth flow on the day.

Parking

You will be required to bring your stall items from your personal transport at the gate, to your designated site. We will have a designated parking area for all stall holders, to free up street parking close to the venue for market goers. You will be notified of this location closer to the date. It will be a short distance, although it will require you to walk back to the venue.

What equipment do I need?

Gazebo: You are required to bring a gazebo for your stall. If you do not have a gazebo, you can purchase one at Bunnings for less than $30, or you could ask a friend, neighbour or relative if you can borrow one for the day. Is there access to power?

No. All stall holders will be located on the oval in market style row format.

Wet weather

We endeavour to go ahead with the Market Day on Saturday November 5th at 1pm. If the weather is exceptionally bad, a decision will be made by 12 noon and you will be notified via a text message and on Facebook. If cancellation on the Saturday is unavoidable, the following day, Sunday November 6th at 1pm, will be our back up option. If a weather front ensures we have to cancel both dates, then the market will be rescheduled for Saturday 19th November at 1pm.
